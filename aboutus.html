About Marine Animal

We have approximately 1000 animals in our places. We always keep our animals in a good condition so you can always see them any time. We have our golden standard to help animals grow much better and also provide a good food and nutritional supplement so they can always eat healthy food. If there's any sick animal we have our highly skilled veterinarian that can keep our animal healty and also every month we always clean up and flush our main tank so, we can always maintan a good ecosystem for the marine animal. In our main tank we use a salt water that comes from sea, so they can easily adapt with the semi sea ecosystem.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q : What time does the Piscis Aquarium open ?

A : We are open at 8 a.m until 12 p.m everyday.

Q : How do i purchase a Piscis Pass?

A : You buy Piscis Pass via Piscis Website or in the Piscis Counter in Piscis Aquarium.

Q : What is the requirement to dive in the main tank?

A : You need to bring your own swim suit, oxygen tube and you also need to pay a diving fee.

Q : Can i bring food or beverages into the aquarium area?

A : No, you cannot bring any kind of food and beverages, if you need a food or beverages you can buy it in the canteen section.

Q : Can i bring a camera ?

A : Yes, you can bring any camera except the professional camera. Also you can use flash in the Aquarium area.
